Tic-Tac-Toe

Let's play tic-tac-toe! This is a simple 2 player game that everyone has played before! In case you forgot the rules (I forget the rules to games often, don't let anyone bully you for forgetting!), each player will play either an X or an O, depending on who goes first. You want to get 3 in a row of the X or O that you are playing. If all boxes are filled up and there are no 3 in a row of either letter, it is a tie game and no one is the winner. Good Luck!
